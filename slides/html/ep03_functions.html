what is a function?

anything that operates on some input

f(x) → y

to determine an output

for example

the area of a circle is a function of its radius

\[ A = \pi r^{2} \] or \[ f(x) = \pi x^{2} \]

mathematically

rather than write out this

\[ \pi 2^{2} = 12.57 \\ \pi 3^{2} = 28.27 \\ \pi 4^{2} = 50.27 \\ ... \]
I can simplify to

\[ f(2) = 12.57 \\ f(3) = 28.27 \\ f(4) = 50.27 \\ ... \]

what does this have to do with coding?

we can use this mathematical abstraction

to encapsulate and execute any arbitrary process at will
